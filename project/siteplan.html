Site Name

Discover Ghana: Explore the Beauty, Culture, and Adventure

This name was chosen to immediately convey the purpose of the site: to showcase Ghana as a destination for tourism. It highlights the key aspects of Ghana that appeal to tourists - natural beauty, rich culture, and adventure opportunities.

Domain Availability: discoverghana.org (Available for registration)

Site Purpose

The purpose of this website is to promote tourism in Ghana by showcasing key tourist destinations to an international audience. The website will serve as a digital travel guide, offering engaging content, cultural insights, and a simulated booking experience for users. It aims to highlight Ghana's natural beauty, historical landmarks, and vibrant culture.

Key objectives include:

  • Showcase Ghana's top tourist destinations
  • Provide cultural insights and travel information
  • Offer a simulated booking experience
  • Educate international visitors about Ghana's heritage
  • Encourage tourism and cultural exchange

Scenarios

These are questions visitors might ask when arriving at the site:

  • What are the must-visit historical sites in Ghana? (Helps drive content about Cape Coast Castle and other heritage locations)
  • How can I experience Ghana's natural parks and wildlife? (Guides content about Mole National Park and Kakum National Park)
  • What cultural festivals happen in Ghana throughout the year? (Informs seasonal content planning)
  • How do I plan a trip that combines beach relaxation with cultural experiences? (Helps create itinerary suggestions)
  • What are the visa requirements for visiting Ghana? (Important practical information for international travelers)

Typography

Two complementary fonts will be used throughout the site:

Montserrat - Headings

This modern, geometric sans-serif font will be used for all headings to create impact and visual hierarchy. Its clean lines make it highly readable at various sizes.

Open Sans - Body Text

This humanist sans-serif font is optimized for web use. Its open forms and neutral appearance make it highly readable for body text and longer passages.

Website Structure

The website will have five pages with the following structure:

  • Home Page: Introduction to Ghanaian tourism with featured destinations
  • Destinations Page: Comprehensive list of tourist sites with filtering options
  • Booking Page: Simulated booking form with validation
  • About/Contact Page: Project background and contact information
  • References Page: Sources for content and images

Technical Implementation

Technologies

  • HTML5 for semantic structure
  • CSS3 with responsive design (mobile-first)
  • JavaScript ES6+ for dynamic content
  • GitHub Pages for hosting

JavaScript Features

  • Dynamic destination listings from JavaScript objects
  • Category filtering for destinations
  • Form validation for booking simulation
  • localStorage for form data persistence
  • Image slider/rotator for homepage
